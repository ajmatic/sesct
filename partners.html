Southeastern
Employment
Services

"We are committed to bringing quality employment services to Southeastern CT because we envision all people working and living healthy, fulfilled lives."

Partner Agencies

From evaluating a person's ability to work, developing resumes, providing job placement and training, to follow-up job coaching and transition services, Southeastern Employment Services (SES) provides career development services that ensure individuals are achieving integrated and competitive employment in their community. This particular type of job development and subsequent support is based on research and ensures that employment is retained for at least ninety days.

SES has been a vendor providing employment services for people with funding from:

  • Bureau of Rehabilitation Services (BRS)
  • Department of Developmental Services (DDS)
  • Department of Social Services
  • Board of Education Services for the Blind (BESB)
  • The Autism Program (DDS)
  • Workers Compensation Commission
  • Public School Districts
  • Private Pay Services
