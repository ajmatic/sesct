Staffing Solutions

Southeastern Employment Services (SES) has been helping area employers with their staffing needs for more than a decade. All of our employment services are provided at no charge to the business. We work in partnership with you and tailor our services to meet your needs. As an employer you will have access to an extensive source of pre-screened applicants who have proven qualifications to meet your specific criteria. You will be able to learn more about a job candidateâ€™s skills in order to evaluate his/her performance first-hand prior to making a job offer.

Benefits to the employer include:

  • Matching job seekers to suitable jobs
  • Helping in training the new employee
  • Offering support to the employee as well as the employer
  • Continually working as a liaison to help problem solve any job related issue, saving you time and money
